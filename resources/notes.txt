Description: Drag n' Drop up to 8 audio files that are automatically assigned to tracks/channels/faders 1-8. gain, panning, dsp can be controlled per track. k-mix transport controls ['play', 'stop', 'back', 'record']. record will save audio output

JS:
	modules: ['midi', 'midi-k-mix', 'samples', 'save-audio', 'web-audio-dsp:Tone/Tuna']
	midi: basic midi connectivity
	midi-k-mix: onmidimessage logic. requires ['web-audio', 'samples', 'Tone/Tuna'] 
	samples: load and export samples
	save-audio: saves audio output, possibly in realtime
	web-audio: global? audio context, 

CSS:
	components: ['k-mix', 'drag-n-drop', 'video']
